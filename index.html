Technical School

In 2023, I enrolled at Parque da Juventude Technical School in São Paulo, where I studied programming-focused subjects. During this period, I gained deeper experience with back-end development, especially due to the technical program’s emphasis on digital game production. Alongside this, I reconnected with front-end development to build my portfolio, creating clean, visually appealing websites and refining them throughout the development process.
